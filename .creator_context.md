# Why this app?
When I was a kid, I simply loved maps. I used to take the empty maps of Canada that our teachers would give us, bring them home, photocopy many copies of the blank map of the whole country, and begin to fill in each city, town, lake, river that I could possibly name.

Later on, when I was in Cégep, I took a course in the geography of the world. As part of that course, we had to learn different parts of every country in the world on a map. In fact, the final exam and almost the entire mark for the course was simply being able to name every single country on a map.

Not only did I find this course rewarding and challenging when I was taking it, but I have found it to be extremely valuable in my life and enriching in the years that followed. Whether that is meeting somebody new and already knowing where the world, the country is, the best thing to be able to instantly, conceptually understand distances and proximities when listening to world news, or simply being able to impress my friends with trivia.

In short, the simple act of memorising all those countries in that course a long time ago has surprisingly enriched my life.  Then, fast forward to my son doing it now a couple of years earlier than when I did it. I suddenly realised that, first of all, I can make the game much more engaging than the ones he was playing.  And secondly, I could make this available to many more people to enrich their lives.
